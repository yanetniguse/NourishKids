Meal Plans

Find sample meal plans tailored to different age groups and dietary needs.

0-6 Months

Exclusive breastfeeding or formula feeding. Gradual introduction of solid foods after 6 months.

6-12 Months

Introduce pureed fruits and vegetables, cereals, and lean proteins. Continue breastfeeding or formula feeding.

1-2 Years

Transition to more solid foods, including small pieces of fruits, vegetables, grains, and proteins. Ensure adequate hydration.

2-5 Years

Provide a variety of foods from all food groups. Focus on whole grains, lean proteins, fruits, and vegetables.
